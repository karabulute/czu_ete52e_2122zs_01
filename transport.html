During the period of its validity the Karlovy VARY REGION CARD can be used as a free public transport ticket in Karlovy Vary and Mariánské Lázně.

Submit the card to transport control on request. It is entitled to check its validity. A passenger with an invalid card risks a fine by the regulations of the Public Transport Company.
