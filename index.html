Welcome to Karlovy Vary

As the principal town on the West Bohemian Spa Triangle and the largest spa complex in Europe, Karlovy Vary has over 80 springs. Although the town of Karlovy Vary was established in 1370 by King Charles IV and was built up during a variety of architectural periods, most of the architecture of the city dates from the Renaissance and later, particularly the period between the late 1800's and 1930.
